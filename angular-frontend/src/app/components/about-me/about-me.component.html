My journey in the world of drones began in summer 2019, when I received as a gift my first drone, the mavic mini. I had never flown any, but it only took a few minutes of the first flight to know that, without looking for it, I had found one of my passions.

Little by little I was gaining experience, at the same time that I was learning to edit videos, a very important part as well.

A short time later I decided to go more seriously with this, and I decided to get rid of my first drone to upgrade to a higher quality one, and that is how in October 2020 I acquired the Mavic Air 2, a drone with better performance, automated tracking functions, hyperlapses ... Anyway, a more than decent drone to do any professional job.

Aware that I wanted to become even more professional, I decided to become a drone pilot. And so it was, in November 2020 I got my AESA certificate as a professional pilot that enables me to fly drones up to 5 kg.

Since then I have recorded and edited several videos, I have recorded and edited a short film and I have participated in others.

Personally, I really enjoy the flight, but I enjoy even more the creative part: thinking of an idea, what I want to convey with it, and how to develop it.
